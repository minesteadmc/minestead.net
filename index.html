ABOUT

Minestead is a vanilla survival multiplayer server on hard difficulty. No ranks, land claims, teleporting, donations, store, or Discord server.

Join a town or venture into the wild, trade with players using diamonds, and build road networks to travel the world. Minestead has a few extra features:

  • Talk with the Simple Voice Chat mod.
  • Build with mini blocks and animal and player heads.
  • Pick the perfect painting and strip logs in the stonecutter.
  • Silence annoying mobs in your farms with a nametag.
  • Always get two shulker shells when raiding end cities.
  • Not every player has to sleep to skip the night.

RULES

Before you can break or place any blocks, you must read the rules at spawn.
  • 1. You must be 16 years or older to play.
  • 2. Everyone belongs here. Be kind: No hate, harassment, discrimination, slurs, or bullying ever! Keep it light, no heavy debate topics like politics or religion.
  • 3. Don't ask for or share personal or identifying information, except Discord usernames. Never record or share public voice chat audio.
  • 4. No griefing or stealing. You may harvest and replant crops unless posted, but do not take items from containers. Do not use xray or other unfair advantages.
  • 5. TNT duping is allowed, but no other dupes including rails, carpets, sand, and gravel. Redstone and chunk loaders should be used sparingly and be lag friendly.
  • 6. No unsolicited PVP or trapping players.
  • 7. Keep chat clean. No spamming, excessive caps, or advertising other servers.
  • 8. Follow staff instructions. All bans are permanent.
